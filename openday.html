🌎 About This Project

Corozal Bay EcoAdventures (CBEA) is a business we joined to promote their concept based on developing our Sustainable Tourism course at CEMJC. Our project promotes responsible tourism in Sarteneja, Corozal District, Belize, where the community can benefit from eco-friendly tourism while protecting the natural environment.

🌎 What We Promote

  • ✔ Mangrove Restoration & conservation of Corozal Bay Wildlife Sanctuary
  • ✔ Snorkelling at Bacalar Chico UNESCO World Heritage Marine Reserve
  • ✔ Traditional PescaTours fishing with Sarteneja's local families
  • ✔ Maya Archaeological Tours at Cerros and Altun Ha
  • ✔ Wildtracks Manatee Rescue Centre visits
  • ✔ Lionfish Jewellery workshops supporting local women artisans
  • ✔ Community-based tourism that keeps 80% of revenue local
