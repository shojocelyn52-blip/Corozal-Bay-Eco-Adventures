Welcome to Corozal Bay EcoAdventures! 🌿

Welcome to Corozal Bay EcoAdventures (CBEA) — Belize's premier community-based ecotourism operator, based in the beautiful coastal village of Sarteneja, Corozal District. CBEA is the ecotourism arm of the Sarteneja Alliance for Conservation and Development (SACD), a community-driven non-profit co-managing the Corozal Bay Wildlife Sanctuary and Bacalar Chico National Park with the Government of Belize. Every experience we offer is authentic, meaningful, and designed to protect the ecosystems and communities you visit — from river to reef. 🍃

🌿 Our Mission & Vision

🌿 Our Mission

To develop a sustainable ecotourism model in the Corozal Bay area that supports the long-term goals of SACD, while ensuring healthy ecosystems from river to reef, fostering resilient communities, and raising awareness through educational experiences.

⭐ Our Vision

To become a leader in responsible ecotourism by offering high-quality transformative experiences that enhance conservation efforts, inspire visitors, and generate sustainable revenue for local communities in the Corozal Bay area.

🌿

Ecological

Protecting mangroves, coral reefs, seagrass beds and the iconic manatee through every experience.

🏭 About Sarteneja, Belize

Sarteneja is one of Belize's most iconic and authentic coastal communities, located on the shores of Corozal Bay in the far north of the country. Once a key Maya trade centre, Sarteneja is today known for its traditional fishing heritage, colourful wooden homes, and the remarkable craft of handmade sailboat building — a skill found nowhere else in Belize. It sits at the gateway to the stunning Bacalar Chico Marine Reserve and the Corozal Bay Wildlife Sanctuary.

🏭 Why Visit Sarteneja?

  • 🌿 Only place in Belize where handmade wooden sailboats are still crafted
  • 🦋 Gateway to the UNESCO World Heritage Bacalar Chico Marine Reserve
  • 🦇 One of few places in Belize to observe manatees in their natural habitat
  • 🏭 Rich Maya heritage — Cerros coastal trade centre located nearby
  • 🎣 Authentic fishing village culture and generations-old traditions
  • 🌿 Vast mangrove ecosystems and Corozal Bay Wildlife Sanctuary
